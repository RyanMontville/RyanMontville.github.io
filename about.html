I'm Ryan, but you probably already knew that since you found my website. I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio and went to school at The Ohio State University. I graduated in 2016 with a bachelors in Communication Technologies with a focus on human-computer interaction. In my free time I like bowling, hanging out with my dog Rufus, and trying new things.

In 2015 I was accepted into the Disney College Program internship in Walt Disney World. I worked on 8 different different teams across multiple lines of businesses including food & beverage, merchandise and attractions. Working for Disney was extremely enjoyable.I became friends with many new people from around the world, I learned many valuable skills, I got to take part in several once-in-a-lifetime events, and made countless unforgettable memories. Everyday brought new and unexpected situations, requiring me to make decisions on the go. Working closely with my team, I strived to keep our location efficient while still creating and delivering those magical experiences to thousands of guests every day.

When I first started the internship, I thought working for Disney would be a temporary thing and that I would go back to living in Ohio after the internship ended. I soon realized that I really enjoyed working for Disney and wanted to make a career out of it. I completed a second internship with Disney after I graduated in 2016, but I enjoyed working there so much, I ended up moving to Florida and working for Disney for the next 5 years.

When I started my first DCP, I started My Adventure Blog to talk about my experience at Disney. If you would like to read more about my time at Disney, click here.

While working for Disney was enjoyable, I have always been interested in programming, especially creating websites. I recently graduated from Tech Elevator's national live remote java bootcamp where I learned how to develop dynamic web-based software systems using the Java programming language and platform. Skills I have learned:

  • Web Application Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Spring Boot, Vue.js, React.js Angular, Wordpress
  • Database Programming: JDBC, Table Design, SQL, PostgreSQL, E/R diagrams
  • Development Tools and Techniques: Agile, Unit Testing (JUnit), Integration Testing, Unix Command Line navigation, Git, IntelliJ, Photoshop, Figma
